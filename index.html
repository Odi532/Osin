Meet Osito: My Favorite Pup!

A glimpse into the life of my loyal, playful Shih Tzu-Pomeranian mix!

About Osito

Osito is my adorable Shih Tzu-Pomeranian mix who has been with me for the past two years. He's a shy but sweet pup who loves snow, playing hide and seek, and getting all the attention. His unique white line on his forehead makes him even more special. Always loyal, Osito waits for me by the door every time I come back home, jumping up with excitement and wagging his tail.

Fun Facts

  • Osito loves to play in the snow and even eats it!
  • He has a white line between his foreheads that makes him extra unique.
  • He enjoys playing hide and seek and sometimes runs away, but always comes back!
  • He's very loyal and waits for me, whether I'm out for a short trip or all day long.
